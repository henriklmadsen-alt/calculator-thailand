## DAILY MONITORING SETUP

### Metrics to Track (Daily)
- **Position:** Current SERP rank (targets: Tier 1 = top 5-10, Tier 2 = top 10-15)
- **Position Change:** Day-over-day delta (alert if drop >3 positions)
- **Impressions:** Monthly trend (from GSC)
- **CTR:** Actual clicks ÷ impressions
- **Opportunity Alert:** New high-intent keywords (vol >100, not yet ranking)

### Tools
- **Primary:** Google Search Console (free, real GSC data)
- **Secondary:** Rank tracking tool (if available: Moz, Ahrefs, or custom script)
- **Frequency:** Daily check (morning UTC, auto-log if possible)

### Alert Thresholds
- **Red Flag:** Tier 1 keyword drops >3 positions in 24h → escalate to CMO
- **Yellow Flag:** Tier 1 keyword new opportunity (vol >500/mo, vol >100/mo for emerging) → flag for article/update planning
- **Green Flag:** Tier 1 keyword moves up 1-2 positions → monitor trend

---

## WEEKLY ON-PAGE REFRESH PROTOCOL

### Monday Audit (Weekly)
1. **Review** Friday's end-of-day position snapshot
2. **Flag** keywords needing action:
   - Position dropped >3 points → content/metadata refresh
   - New opportunity keywords → article or calculator support needed
   - CTR <3% despite good ranking → metadata optimization
3. **Prioritize** top 10 Tier 1 keywords for immediate action
4. **Route** to Thai Content Specialist Alpha + SEO Specialist for execution

### Refresh Actions
- **Metadata Refresh:** Title, meta description, H1 alignment to keyword intent
- **Content Update:** Article support, new examples, FAQ expansion
- **Internal Linking:** Add/update links from related calculators, articles
- **Schema Review:** Ensure FAQPage, HowTo, SoftwareApplication markup present

### Success Criteria for Refresh
- Target: +2 average position improvement within 2 weeks
- CTR improvement: 3-5% within 4 weeks post-refresh
- Click volume: +10-15% within 30 days of article/update launch
