## Quick-Start Guide for 300+ Page Audit

**Purpose:** Assess and improve search-result framing on all calculator pages  
**Scope:** Title tags and meta descriptions  
**Priority order:** Financial calculators first → Insurance → Healthcare → Others  
**Timeline:** 2-3 weeks to audit all 300+; start with top 50

---

## What We're Auditing

### Title Tag Standards
Each title should:
- ✅ Contain the **Thai query people actually search** (e.g., "เครื่องคำนวณสินเชื่อบ้าน" for mortgage calc)
- ✅ Be **specific, not generic** (e.g., "Car Loan Calculator" is weak; "เครื่องคำนวณสินเชื่อรถ - คำนวณสัดส่วนเงินกู้" is strong)
- ✅ Be **under 60 characters** (to avoid truncation in search results)
- ✅ **Match the page's actual purpose** (not clickbait, not overpromised)
- ❌ NOT generic ("Calculator", "Tool", "Online")
- ❌ NOT misleading (don't promise what page doesn't deliver)
- ❌ NOT filled with keywords unnecessarily

### Meta Description Standards
Each description should:
- ✅ Summarize **what the calculator does** in plain Thai
- ✅ Be **useful to the searcher** (what value do they get?)
- ✅ Be **155-160 characters** (fits in search results without truncation)
- ✅ Be **specific to this calculator** (not the same description as 10 others)
- ✅ Include a **call-to-action** if appropriate ("คำนวณทันที" / "ลองใช้ตอนนี้")
- ❌ NOT generic ("This is a calculator")
- ❌ NOT copy-paste from other pages
- ❌ NOT overpromising (don't claim accuracy you don't have)

---

## Audit Checklist

For each calculator, rate it on this scale:

| Rating | Title | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| 🟢 Strong | Specific Thai intent + brand combo; <60 chars | Unique summary; 155-160 chars; specific value prop |
| 🟡 Mixed | Some intent match; could be more specific | Generic or truncated; needs work |
| 🔴 Weak | Generic or non-Thai; missing intent | Placeholder or cookie-cutter; low appeal |

## Common Metadata Mistakes to Avoid

### 🔴 Too Generic
❌ "Calculator"  
❌ "Online Tool"  
❌ "Calculate Your Number"  
✅ "เครื่องคำนวณสินเชื่อบ้าน - คำนวณจำนวนเงินกู้ รวมดอกเบี้ย"

### 🔴 Misleading
❌ "Guaranteed Accurate" (you can't guarantee it)  
❌ "Best Mortgage Calculator" (superlatives without proof)  
❌ "Free, No Limits" (if there are limits)  
✅ "Mortgage Calculator - Estimate Monthly Payments Quickly"

### 🔴 Copy-Paste
❌ Same description on 10 different calculators  
❌ Generic template language (e.g., "Use our calculator to...")  
✅ Unique description per calculator; specific to its purpose

### 🔴 Wrong Language
❌ "Mortgage สินเชื่อบ้าน" (mixing Thai and English awkwardly)  
❌ Long English phrases in Thai context  
✅ Thai-first titles; English only if necessary for clarity

## Execution Steps

### Step 1: Pick Your Tier
Start with **Tier 1 (Financial)** — highest search volume, highest intent.

### Step 2: Audit Sample (5-10 Pages)
Spend 1-2 hours auditing the top 5 calculators.  
Document ratings and recommended changes.  
Share with Thai Content Specialist for language review.

### Step 3: Get Template Approval
Before auditing all 300, get one completed template approved by:
- SEO Specialist (intent fit, quality standards)
- Thai Content Specialist (language, tone)
- CTO (implementation path)

### Step 4: Bulk Audit
With template approved, audit remaining pages at scale.  
Target: 20 pages per day (15 hours total for all 300).

### Step 5: Implementation Queue
Sort by priority:
- 🔴 Weak metadata on high-value calculators (do first)
- 🟡 Mixed metadata (do second)
- 🟢 Strong metadata (spot-check for drift, then skip)

### Step 6: Deploy & Verify
- Bulk update titles/descriptions in codebase
- Verify in published site
- Wait for Google re-crawl (24-48 hours)
- Monitor Google Search Console for click quality changes
