## Framework: Article Support Decision Criteria

### Article Support Needed IF:
- ✅ High search intent (100+ monthly estimated searches)
- ✅ User needs context or background (not instantly clear)
- ✅ Calculator supports business/financial decision (needs guidance)
- ✅ Cluster opportunity (article can link related calculators)
- ✅ Multiple related articles possible (topic cluster sustainable)

### Standalone (Article Optional) IF:
- ✅ Ultra-clear purpose (BMI, basic percentage, time zone)
- ✅ Low learning curve (no guidance needed)
- ✅ Universal tool (works for everyone instantly)
- ✅ Trust already obvious (inherent credibility)
- ✅ No natural cluster (article wouldn't add value)
