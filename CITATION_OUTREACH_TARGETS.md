## Strategy Overview

### Why These Three Categories?

1. **Thai Government (.gov.th)**
   - Highest domain authority (DA 70+)
   - SEO trust signal = strong
   - Relevant: tax education, financial literacy, public resources
   - Citations appear in search results as "official guidance"

2. **Thai Universities (.ac.th)**
   - High domain authority (DA 60+)
   - Relevant: economics, finance, business education
   - Research resources for students/faculty
   - Long-term backlinks (seldom removed)

3. **Thai Business Media**
   - Domain authority: 40–55
   - Relevance: finance, tax, business news
   - Referral traffic + backlink + credibility
   - Reach: decision-makers, business owners, professionals
