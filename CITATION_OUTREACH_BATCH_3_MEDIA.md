## Key Success Factors

1. **Personalize journalist names** — "Dear [Name]" not generic
2. **Lead with story angle** — Journalists pitch stories, not tools
3. **Multiple options** — Give 3–4 angle choices
4. **Quick response promised** — "Happy to discuss..."
5. **Data/quotes available** — "Can provide expert quotes, data"
6. **Timing awareness** — Tax season (March), year-end planning (November)
