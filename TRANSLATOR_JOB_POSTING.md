## About Calculator Thailand

Calculator Thailand (kamnuanlek.com) is Thailand's #1 free Thai-language calculator platform. We help Thai users quickly solve everyday math problems—tax calculations, loan payments, electricity bills, property transfers, and more. We're expanding to English to serve the growing overseas Thai community and international residents in Thailand.

**Company scale**: 500K USD annual revenue, 150K+ monthly organic users, AdSense-powered monetization.

---

## The Project

**Scope**: Translate 6 core calculator pages + supporting explanatory content from Thai to clear, natural English.

**Content to translate**:
1. **Electricity Bill Calculator** (ค่าไฟฟ้า) — utility billing, tiered rates
2. **Land & Building Tax** (ภาษีโอนที่ดินและสิ่งปลูกสร้าง) — property transfer tax
3. **Income Tax Calculator** (อากรรายได้) — personal income tax, deductions, MDR
4. **Loan Payment Calculator** (การชำระเงินกู้) — amortization, interest
5. **Overtime Pay Calculator** (ค่าตรวจสอบการทำงานนอกเวลา) — labor law, compensation
6. **Property Transfer Tax** (ภาษีการสืบทอด) — inheritance, estate tax

**Estimated word count**: ~40,000–50,000 words total (calculator explanations, FAQs, examples, metadata)

**Content style**: Clear, helpful, trustworthy, non-legal (disclaimers present). Tone: "helpful friend," not "tax authority."

## What We're Looking For

### Language Skills
- **Native Thai speaker** or near-native fluency (born/raised in Thailand or equivalent exposure)
- **Advanced English** — writing for non-expert audiences, clear explanations
- **No Thai-to-English translation required** (we have Thai originals); you translate TO English
- **Cultural awareness**: Understanding Thai financial/tax context + ability to explain it to English speakers unfamiliar with Thai system

### Domain Expertise
- **Financial/tax background** strongly preferred (accounting, banking, tax compliance, bookkeeping)
- Experience translating financial or legal documents
- Familiarity with Thai tax terminology (หักลดหย่อน, MDR, Social Security contributions, property tax)
- Ability to research and clarify unclear terminology with original authors

### Practical Requirements
- **Available to start**: May 1–2, 2026
- **Timeline**: Full project completion by May 19, 2026 (~19 days)
- **Revision rounds**: 2–3 rounds of feedback/refinement included
- **Communication**: Weekly check-ins, respond within 24–48 hours to questions
- **Deliverables**: 
  - Google Sheets or Excel glossary (term consistency tracking)
  - Draft translations in markdown or HTML
  - Final proofread version
  - Notes on ambiguous terms or cultural adaptation choices
