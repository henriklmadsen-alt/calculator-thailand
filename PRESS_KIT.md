## Frequently Asked Questions (for Media)

### General

**Q: Is Kamnuanlek.com affiliated with any bank or financial institution?**  
A: No. We are independent and free from financial institution control. Our mission is to provide accurate, unbiased calculation tools.

**Q: How do you ensure calculator accuracy?**  
A: Every formula is verified against official Thai tax codes, interest rate standards, and mathematical best practices before publication. We cite sources and update formulas as laws change.

**Q: Is Kamnuanlek.com safe to use?**  
A: Yes. We use SSL encryption, conduct no user tracking on calculations, and keep all data private.

**Q: How do you monetize?**  
A: Through Google AdSense advertising. This allows us to keep all calculators free for users.

**Q: Can my publication use your calculators?**  
A: Yes. You may embed or link to our calculators in your articles. Attribution and linking back to Kamnuanlek.com is appreciated.
