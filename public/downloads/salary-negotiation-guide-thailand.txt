SECTION 6: NEGOTIATION STRATEGY & TIPS
======================================

BEFORE THE MEETING:

☐ Practice your pitch (ซ้อมการพูด 3-5 ครั้ง)
☐ Prepare 3-5 talking points หลัก
☐ Know your walk-away number
☐ Dress professionally (formal attire)
☐ Bring 1 copy of your CV / achievements
☐ Arrive 5-10 นาที ก่อนเวลา


DURING NEGOTIATION:

☐ Start with gratitude: "Thank you for this opportunity. I'm excited about..."
☐ Lead with value, not need: "I bring X to the role..."
☐ Use silence effectively: Don't rush to accept first offer
☐ Ask clarifying questions: "Can you break down that offer?"
☐ Anchor high (10-15% above target): Employer will counter
☐ Focus on total package: Not just base salary
☐ Get everything in writing: Don't rely on verbal promises


OBJECTION HANDLING:

If employer says: "That's above our budget"
⇒ Response: "What budget were you working with? How can we bridge the gap?
            [offer: deferred increase, performance bonus, or specific benefits]"

If employer says: "You don't have enough experience"
⇒ Response: "I understand. I've successfully delivered [specific example]. Can we
            start at [lower number] with a review in 6 months?"

If employer says: "We can't offer that much"
⇒ Response: "What CAN you offer? [Listen]. Can we include [specific benefit]
            to close the gap? Or discuss a 6-month/annual review?"

If employer says: "Take it or leave it"
⇒ Response: "I appreciate the offer. Can I have 24-48 hours to consider?"
            [Then decide: accept, walk away, or propose counter-offer]

SECTION 9: RED FLAGS & WARNINGS
================================

⚠️ DO NOT ACCEPT:
☐ "We'll discuss salary after 3 months" (unless you must)
☐ Salary contingent on 100% performance (too vague)
☐ No written offer (get it in writing immediately)
☐ Bait-and-switch (offered one thing, told another day 1)
☐ No benefits at all (be very careful)

⚠️ NEGOTIATE FURTHER IF:
☐ Offered < your minimum acceptable salary
☐ No health insurance included
☐ No paid time off
☐ Undefined bonus structure ("we'll figure it out")
☐ Drastically different from market rate


CHECKLIST BEFORE ACCEPTING
===========================

☐ Salary meets or exceeds minimum acceptable: __________ บาท
☐ Bonus/benefits package is clear in writing
☐ Understand probation period (typically 3-6 months)
☐ Know when next salary review happens
☐ Understand what "performance metrics" mean for raises
☐ Have signed offer letter with all details
☐ Understand tax implications (see tax calculator: www.kamnuanlek.com)
☐ Feel good about the role and team


FINAL TIPS
==========

☐ Always negotiate (95% of offers have room)
☐ Numbers first, benefits second (base salary is hardest to change later)
☐ Get everything in writing
☐ Propose solutions, not problems ("Can we include X instead?")
☐ Know your walk-away number
☐ Don't be emotional (this is business)
☐ Thank them, even if you decline
☐ Remember: You have more power than you think
