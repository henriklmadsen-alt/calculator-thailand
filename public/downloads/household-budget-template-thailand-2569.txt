HOW TO USE THIS TEMPLATE:

1. Print this template (or use digital version)
2. Fill in your income from primary sources
3. List all fixed expenses (rent, insurance, etc.)
4. Track variable expenses throughout the month
5. Add savings/investment goals
6. Calculate total income vs. total expenses
7. Identify areas to reduce or improve
8. Review monthly and adjust as needed
9. Do quarterly and annual reviews
10. Update savings goals each year

This template helps you understand where your money goes,
identify savings opportunities, and work toward financial goals.
