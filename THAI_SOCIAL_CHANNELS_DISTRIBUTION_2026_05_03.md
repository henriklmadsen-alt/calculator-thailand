## Overview

These 20 channels represent the highest-engagement Thai-language communities for financial, personal finance, and utility calculator content. Prioritized by:
- **Relevance**: How well financial/practical calculators fit the community
- **Audience Size**: Active members/followers
- **Engagement Rate**: Typical post engagement (shares, comments, saves)
- **Posting Rules**: Restrictions on promotional content
- **Growth Potential**: Likelihood of viral sharing
