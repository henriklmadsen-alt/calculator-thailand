## Key Contact Protocol

### Before Sending Email
1. Verify contact email is correct
2. Research organization website for tone/messaging clues
3. Personalize subject line and opening
4. Include specific calculator recommendations
5. Double-check grammar/Thai name pronunciation

### After Positive Response
1. Send thank you email immediately
2. Provide any additional information requested
3. Ask for timeline to publication/linking
4. Offer to help with any questions
5. Mark as "cited" once link goes live
6. Send follow-up thank you once link is live

### After No Response (7-day follow-up)
1. Send light reminder email
2. Offer additional value (e.g., new calculator announcement)
3. Keep tone friendly and helpful (not pushy)
4. If still no response at 14 days, consider campaign closed for this contact

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Low response rate | Fewer backlinks | Personalize emails, multiple follow-ups, quality pitches |
| Email bounces | Missing targets | Verify emails before sending, use multiple contact methods if needed |
| Slow publication timeline (media) | Delayed backlinks | Start media outreach early, follow up regularly |
| Quality drops | Lower SEO value | Focus on DA 40+ sites only, track domain authority |
| Competitor citations | Not unique | Position as unique educational resource, not competing tool |
