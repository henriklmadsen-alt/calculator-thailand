## PART 3: CRITICAL RISKS (What Can Slow Growth)

### Risk 1: Weak Internal Linking Prevents Cluster Depth
**Impact:** Medium | **Likelihood:** High | **Timeline:** Now through June

Calculators exist, but without linking them to supporting articles, users calculate once and leave. No discovery of deeper content → lower ad impressions per session.

**Mitigation:** Implement systematic internal-linking improvement (20-30 key links over 2 weeks).

### Risk 2: Metadata Not Optimized for CTR
**Impact:** Medium | **Likelihood:** Medium | **Timeline:** May-June

Current metadata is honest but not CTR-optimized. On competitive queries, weak click-through rate = fewer visitors.

**Mitigation:** A/B test and improve titles/descriptions on 20-30 highest-opportunity pages.

### Risk 3: Article Clusters Not Driving Monetization
**Impact:** Medium | **Likelihood:** High | **Timeline:** Through June

We have 62+ support articles but they're not connected to calculators. Articles alone have low CPM; calculators have high CPM. Isolated articles = revenue waste.

**Mitigation:** Link articles → calculators bidirectionally to drive traffic toward monetized pages.

### Risk 4: Organic Traffic Growth Plateau
**Impact:** High | **Likelihood:** Medium | **Timeline:** June-July

If we rely only on existing page visibility without improving discoverability and linking, traffic growth will slow to ~10-15% month-over-month. To hit 3.3x by August, we need 20-25% MoM growth.

**Mitigation:** Execute aggressive cluster-support and discoverability improvements Q2.

### If Metadata Optimization Underperforms

**Scenario:** CTR increases only 5% instead of 10-15%.

**Mitigation Actions:**
1. Test more aggressive titles (expand CTR testing scope)
2. Review competitor landing pages; copy winning patterns
3. Focus on queries where we rank 3-5 (highest CTR improvement potential)
4. Evaluate search intent mismatches; reposition weak-fit pages

### If New Articles Don't Drive Traffic

**Scenario:** Phase 3 articles get indexed but don't drive meaningful calculator traffic.

**Mitigation Actions:**
1. Evaluate article quality and keyword targeting (may need rewrites)
2. Strengthen internal linking (more pathways to calculators)
3. Improve metadata on articles for better search visibility
4. Consider consolidating weak articles; focus writing on high-opportunity topics
