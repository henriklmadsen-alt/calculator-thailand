## Thai Quality Notes

### Natural Tone
- Benefit copy is **direct, not salesy** (ตอบเรื่องการเงินส่วนตัว = "answer personal finance" not "get personal finance advice service")
- Taglines use **conversational Thai** (เหมาะส่วนตัว, ทุกวัน, ไม่จำกัด)
- CTA buttons are **action-oriented** (อัปเกรด, เลือก, สมัครสมาชิก)
- No **machine-translation markers** ("support for", "feature of", awkward verb phrasing)

### Readability
- Each benefit is **one concept** (breaks into 1-2 lines on mobile)
- No **redundancy** (benefits don't repeat across tiers except where meaningful)
- **Pricing is clear** (฿399/เดือน vs ฿3,990/ปี format is consistent)
- **Emoji** (⭐) enhances Premium visual hierarchy without cluttering

### Trust & Clarity
- **Free tier is honest** (3 คำถาม is clear, no hidden limits)
- **Benefits match limits** (200 q/month tier says "ทุกวัน" not "unlimited")
- **CTAs are clear** (no vague "Subscribe now", specific tier names in buttons)
- **Annual discount is called out** (ประหยัด 17% gives concrete reason to choose annual)
