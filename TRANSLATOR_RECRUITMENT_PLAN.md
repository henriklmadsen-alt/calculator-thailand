## CANDIDATE SCREENING CHECKLIST (2026-04-29)

### Phone Screen Questions (20 min each)

**Background** (5 min):
- [ ] How many years translating Thai-English?
- [ ] Financial/tax translation experience? (show portfolio)
- [ ] Biggest challenge you've faced translating financial terms?

**This Project** (10 min):
- [ ] Can you start May 1? How many hours/day?
- [ ] Familiar with Thai tax terminology (MDR, Social Security, property tax)?
- [ ] How do you handle terminology consistency across large projects?
- [ ] Would you use a glossary if provided? (Test: "Do you find glossaries helpful?")
- [ ] What's your revision process? (How many rounds?)

**Logistics** (5 min):
- [ ] Preferred payment method (Upwork, PayPal, bank transfer)?
- [ ] Any concerns with $2,500–3,500 budget and May 1–19 timeline?
- [ ] Questions for us?

### Red Flags
- [ ] Vague about tax terminology ("I'll figure it out")
- [ ] No portfolio or references
- [ ] Poor English writing (grammatical errors in emails/application)
- [ ] Unwilling to use glossary or request consistency
- [ ] Availability concerns ("Maybe I can work part-time")
- [ ] Price negotiation immediately ($5K+ demands)

### Green Flags
- [ ] Mentions prior financial translation projects
- [ ] Asks clarifying questions about glossary/tone
- [ ] Confident about Thai tax system
- [ ] Agrees to revision rounds
- [ ] Professional communication
- [ ] Available to start May 1, full commitment

Calculator Thailand — Financial Calculator Translation Project

1. SCOPE
   Translator agrees to translate [6 calculator pages + supporting content] 
   from Thai to English (~40,000–50,000 words total)

2. TIMELINE
   - Start date: May 1, 2026
   - Final delivery: May 19, 2026
   - Revision rounds: 2–3 included in fee

3. COMPENSATION
   - Total fee: $[2,500–3,500 USD]
   - Payment: 50% upfront (upon signature), 50% upon final delivery
   - Payment method: [PayPal/Bank Transfer/Upwork]

4. DELIVERABLES
   - Glossary (Thai ↔ English terms)
   - Draft translations (all calculators)
   - Revised translations (per feedback)
   - Final proofread version
   - QA notes on terminology choices

5. IP & RIGHTS
   - All translations owned by Calculator Thailand
   - Translator agrees not to republish or resell translations
   - Full usage rights to Calculator Thailand (website, archives, monetization)

6. COMMUNICATION
   - Weekly check-ins (email/Slack)
   - Response time: 24–48 hours on questions
   - Escalation: Direct contact with CMO

7. REVISIONS
   - 2–3 revision rounds included
   - Additional revisions: $50 USD per round
   - Feedback provided within 48 hours

8. TERMINATION
   - Either party may terminate with 48-hour notice
   - Translator paid for completed work pro-rated
   - Forfeited deposit applied to final payment

9. CONFIDENTIALITY
   - Translator agrees not to share translations publicly until published

10. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR
    - Translator is independent contractor (not employee)
    - Responsible for own taxes and benefits

AGREED:
Translator: _______________________     Date: _______
Calculator Thailand CMO: __________     Date: _______
